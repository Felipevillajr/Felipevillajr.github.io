Maya Angelou

The Life and Times

    Born Marguerite Annie Johnson in April 4th, 1928 Published & autobiographies, three books of essays, several books of poetry Received many awards and held more than 50 honorary degrees She worked as a cook, sex worker, nightclub performer, Porgy and Bess cast member, Southern Christian Leadership Conference coordinator, and correspondent in Eqypt and Ghana. Actress, writer, director, and producer of plays, movies and television programs Known in writing for challenging the common structure of autobiography Her works centered on themes about racism, identity, family and travel died May 28th, 2014
